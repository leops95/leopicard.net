About me

I’m a doctoral student and assistant at the University of Basel within the Department of Public Economics. My research focuses on the effect of climate policy on firms’ behavior.

I also enjoy working with my colleagues from ETH Zürich in the Law, Economics, and Data Science group. More precisely, we study media rhetorics and the role of language in the political arena.
